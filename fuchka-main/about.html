About Us

About Company

Fuchka Delight began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors of Bangladesh street food to food enthusiasts around the world. Founded in 2024 by Tahamed, our journey started as a small food cart in the heart of the city.

Tahamid up in Bogura, where he developed a passion for the vibrant street food culture. After moving abroad and noticing the lack of authentic Fuchka, he decided to share his family's secret recipes with the world.

What started as a humble street cart has now grown into a beloved restaurant, serving thousands of happy customers. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our founding principles: authentic flavors, quality ingredients, and exceptional service.
