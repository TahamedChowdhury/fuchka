What is Pani Puri?

Pani Puri is a popular Indian street food that consists of a hollow, crispy shell (puri) filled with a mixture of flavored water (pani), tamarind chutney, chaat masala, potato, onion, and chickpeas. It's known for its explosion of flavors when you eat it in one bite.

Are your Pani Puris vegetarian?

Yes, our Pani Puris are 100% vegetarian. In fact, most of our menu items are vegetarian. We also have vegan options available upon request.

How spicy is your Pani Puri?

We offer Pani Puri in various spice levels ranging from mild to extra spicy. You can specify your preferred spice level when ordering. If you're trying Pani Puri for the first time, we recommend starting with the mild version.

Do you use any preservatives in your food?

No, we don't use any artificial preservatives in our food. All our dishes are prepared fresh daily using natural ingredients. Our chutneys and fillings are made in-house using traditional recipes.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 5-mile radius of our restaurant. You can place your order through our website, by phone, or through popular food delivery apps. There's a minimum order of $15 for delivery.

How do I place a catering order?

For catering orders, please contact us at least 48 hours in advance. You can call us directly at +91 98765 43210 or fill out the catering form on our website. Our team will get back to you with a customized quote based on your requirements.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

You can modify your order within 10 minutes of placing it. Please call us directly to make any changes. After 10 minutes, we start preparing your food, and modifications may not be possible.

Do you offer private dining or event spaces?

Yes, we have a private dining area that can accommodate up to 20 people. It's perfect for small gatherings, celebrations, or business meetings. Please contact us at least one week in advance to reserve the space.

Do you offer cooking classes?

Yes, we conduct cooking classes once a month where Chef Raj teaches how to prepare various Indian street foods, including Pani Puri. The classes are interactive and include all materials. Check our Events page for upcoming class dates and registration details.

What are your business hours?

We are open seven days a week from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Our kitchen closes at 9:30 PM, so please place your last orders by then.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking lot available for our customers. Additionally, there's street parking available around the restaurant. During peak hours, we offer complimentary valet service.

Are you open on holidays?

We are open on most holidays, though our hours may vary. Please check our website or social media for holiday hour announcements. We are closed on Diwali and Christmas Day.
